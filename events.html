The Mehndi-Ki-Raat (Night of Henna) signifies beautification before a wedding ceremony. The Sangeet (Music) is a celebration of song and dance.

We combine these two traditional events along with a feast of food and drink at a private oceanfront estate. Ladies, henna artists will be available to paint your hands.

Transportation to/from hotels will be provided and will depart at 4:00 PM

Dress: Traditional Indian dress encouraged. The more color the better!
